Controls

  • For the Best Experience in Your Browser Place the Zoom at 80% unless you are using a 4k screen or play in fullscreen mode in your brower (F11)
  • Use the WASD, Arrow Keys or the Mouse to Move the Bug Zapper
  • Press the Spacebar, Enter or Left-click with the mouse to fire the Zapper

Gameplay Instructions

  • Your Main object is to defeat the Centipede to progress to the next level.
  • There are other enemies as Well so watch out!
  • Getting touched by any of the bugs will cost you a life
  • Lose all lives and its a game over!

Gameplay Tips

  • To Survive the longest killing just the bugs onscreen wont be enough, shooting the mushrooms will award power ups
  • Dont Stop Moving there are enemies that can bounce to the floor upon entering the game
  • Unlike the original game you can move past throughout the game field but be warned extra space does not give you more saftey just more breathing room
  • If you do loose a life you will be given a shield to protect you for around 8 seconds
  • You will earn an Extra Life every 10000 points

Centipede

    Enemies

  • Centipede:

    • The main enemy of the game hitting it will cause it to split apart and move independently from the orginal body killing it completely will start a new level. Health: 1 Score: 10 per Segment 20 for Head

  • Spider:

    • The Spider will hover towards the player tracking them endlessly, Killing it closer to you rewards more points. Health: 1 Score: 300, 600 ,900

  • Flea:

    • Drops from the ceiling down to the floor spawning mushrooms on the way, hitting it will cause it to drop faster to the bottom. Health: 2 Score: 200

  • Scorpion:

    • moves across the field poisoning any mushrooms it touches! Poisoned Mushrooms causes erratic behavior in enemies! Health: 1 Score: 1000

  • Wasp:

    • Moves across the field similar to the centipede hitting it causes it to move even faster! Health: 3 Score: 500

  • Jumping Spider:

    • Moves across the field in 45 degree angles shooting it makes it jump around faster it can instantly pounce on a player as it enters the game! Armor: 1 Health: 3 Score: 600, 900, 1200

  • Armored Scorpion:

    • Moves across the field tracking the player , poisons mushrooms even faster than normal Armor: 4 Health: 4 Score: 2000

  • Lightning Wasp:

    • Moves across the field in a diagonal, shooting it will cause it to fly around bouncing off walls at an alarming rate Armor: 1 Health: 3 Score: 1000

    Other Entities

  • Mushrooms:

    • Shoot them to give power ups, poisoned mushrooms can poison enemies causing erratic and dangerous behavior, respawns periodically, can block both player and enemy movement. Health: 4 Score: 10 per hit, 50, 100 if Poisoned
