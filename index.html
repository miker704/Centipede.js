Centipede

Controls

  • Use the WASD, Arrow Keys or the Mouse to Move the Bug Zapper
  • Press the Spacebar, Enter or Left-click with the mouse to fire the Zapper

Gameplay Instructions

  • Your Main object is to defeat the Centipede to progress to the next level.
  • There are other enemies as Well so watch out! There Spiders that will chase you endlessly among other creatures
  • Getting touched by any of the bugs will cost you a life
  • Lose all lives and its a game over!

Further Gameplay Information and Tips

  • To Survive the longest killing just the bugs onscreen wont be enough, shooting the mushrooms will award small orbs similar in size to your bullets these are power ups
  • Do not Stop Moving there are enemies that can bounce around to the floor upon entering the game
  • Unlike the original game you can move past throughout the game field but be warned extra space does not give you much saftey just a bit of breathing room
  • If you do loose a life you will be given a shield to protect you for around 8 seconds
  • You will earn an Extra Life every 10000 points

Enemies

  • Centipede:

    • The main enemy of the game hitting it will cause it to split apart and move independently from the orginal body 10 points per body segment 20 for head killing a centipede completely will start a new level. Health: 4 Score:

  • Spider:

    • The Spider will appear and hover towards the player it will track the player endlessly reagrdless of where you go, Killing it closer to you rewards more points. Health: 1 Score: 300, 600 ,900

  • Flea:

    • Spawns Periodically drops from the ceiling down to the player spawning mushrooms on its way down hitting it will cause it to move lightning quick to the bottom so be careful as the mushrooms it spawns provide it cover as you shoot it! Health: 2 Score: 200

  • Scorpion:

    • moves across the field poisoning any mushrooms it touches! Poisoned Mushrooms cause erratic behavior to enemies that touch it ! Health: 1 Score: 1000

  • Wasp:

    • Moves across the field similar to the centipede hitting it will cause it to move even faster so watch out! Health: 3 Score: 500

  • Jumping Spider:

    • Moves across the field in 45 degree angles be warned shooting it makes it bounce and jump around faster can instantly pounce on a player as it enters the game! Armor: 1 Health: 3 Score: 600, 900, 1200

  • Armored Scorpion:

    • Moves across the field tracking the player it can poison mushrooms even faster than the normal variant its reccomended to deal with it as soon as it enters the game as it will take multiple shots to put down Armor: 4 Health: 4 Score: 2000

  • Lightning Wasp:

    • Moves across the field in a diagonal like fashion shooting it will cause it to fly around bouncing off walls at an alarming rate Armor: 1 Health: 3 Score: 1000

    Other Entities
