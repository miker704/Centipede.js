Centipede

Controls

  • Use the WASD, Arrow Keys or the Mouse to Move
  • Pressing the Spacebar, Enter or right-clicking with the mouse to fire the Zapper

Gameplay Instructions

  • Your Main object is to defeat the Centipede to progress to level.
  • There are other enemies as Well watch out for the Spiders they will chase you endlessly
  • Getting touched by any of the bugs will cost you a life
  • Lose all lives and its a game over!
